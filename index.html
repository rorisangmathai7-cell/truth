Welcome to Basotho cultural heritage web. Basotho cultural heritage is a rich and vibrant history shaped by the mountainous geography of the Basotho people in Lesotho. In the early 19th century, the Basotho nation was born through the help of King Moshoeshoe at Thaba Bosiu.

In this web, we are going to talk about the oral traditions of the Basotho people and how culture represents a dynamic and unified heritage.
